Description

The Noor is a floor-length double-breasted dress crafted from a structured wool crepe, finished with hand-set crystal buttons running the full length of the bodice. Sheer organza collar and cuffs detailed with hand-applied flocked dots add a couture flourish without breaking the silhouette.

Designed and finished at our Kuwait atelier. Each piece carries a single white stitch on the inside seam — our maker's signature.

Fabric & Materials
